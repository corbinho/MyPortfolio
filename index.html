My Name is Corbin and I am a Software Developer/Revenue Accountant. I currently work at ServiceNow as a Senior Revenue Accountant, where I am heavily involved in contract review and deal structuring. During the last 5 years working at ServiceNow, I have taken part in many new solution implementations, for our own software and SAP Software. My role mostly consisted of design of test plans, user acceptance testing (UAT), and bug fixes. During these implementations, I have found many times there are disconnects between the customer (the business desiring the solution) and the developers of said solution. This disconnect causes added UAT, a solution which may not perform as expected, an additional development work.

To bridge this gap, I am leveraging my previous business knowledge, and completing a Coding Bootcamp at UC San Diego Extension. During this bootcamp, we are learning the MERN stack, with great emphasis on JS, Express, React, nodeJS and MongoDB. At the completion of this bootcamp, I desire to move onto a role which would be able to bridge this disconnect between the end user and engineer. Knowing both the business side of the house: business requirement documentation, process flows, UAT and the development side would allow me to hit the ground running in any Software Engineer Role.
